# State Farm

State Farm is the largest property and casualty insurance provider in the United States, headquartered in Bloomington, Illinois. Founded in 1922, the company offers auto, home, renters, life, health, business, and farm/ranch insurance, as well as banking and financial services. State Farm operates through approximately 19,000 exclusive agents and is a mutual company owned by its policyholders, consistently ranked among the Fortune 50. State Farm's Partner Gateway developer portal at developer.statefarm.com exposes APIs enabling partners to embed State Farm insurance products into their applications.

**Website:** [https://www.statefarm.com](https://www.statefarm.com)  
**Developer Portal:** [https://developer.statefarm.com](https://developer.statefarm.com)

## APIs

### Renters Insurance API

Enables partner platforms, real estate applications, and property management systems to offer embedded renters insurance quotes and policies to tenants. State Farm is the industry leader in renters insurance in the US.

- **Documentation:** [https://developer.statefarm.com/api/renters](https://developer.statefarm.com/api/renters)
- **OpenAPI:** [openapi/state-farm-renters-insurance-openapi.yml](openapi/state-farm-renters-insurance-openapi.yml)

### Auto Insurance API

Supports partner integrations for automobile insurance quoting, policy management, and claims inquiry. Enables auto dealers, telematics platforms, and financial institutions to embed State Farm auto insurance.

- **Documentation:** [https://developer.statefarm.com](https://developer.statefarm.com)

### B2B Insurance Inquiry API

Designed for lenders and mortgage servicers to verify homeowner and auto insurance policy status for collateral protection purposes.

- **Documentation:** [https://b2b.statefarm.com/b2b-content/home-auto-lenders/ins-inquiry](https://b2b.statefarm.com/b2b-content/home-auto-lenders/ins-inquiry)
